Interschool Campers Capers (music category)
Won third prize in Interschool Campers Capers (music category)

In the music category, Reqelford’s band bagged the third prize. The talented ReqelBand, which has performed on various occasions in the school, wowed the audiences by putting up a stellar show on the drums, guitar, piano and vocals. Our students proudly represented Reqelford in the event. Summing things up in the words of our Head Boy, “The competitions were great!”

REQUIZ Interschool Competition
Won first prize in REQUIZ Interschool Competition

Reqelford International School ranked 1st, Tejaswi Vidhyaranya School 2nd and Indus Universal School 3rd. In the Senior category, St. Joseph’s School took the 1st prize followed by Army Public School 2nd and St. Peter’s Model School in the 3rd place. Certificates, mementos and prizes were given during the Prize Distribution Ceremony. REQUIZ was indeed a very prestigious event at the Reqelford campus.
